Basic Chocolate Cake

What You'll Need


Basic Chocolate Cake Recipe
  • 1 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup White Sugar
  • 125g butter
  • 2 Tbsp Golden Syrup
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
